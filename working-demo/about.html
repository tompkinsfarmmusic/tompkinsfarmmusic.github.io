    What is Tompkins Farm Music?

    Tompkins Farm Music is a boutique music publisher located in Westchester County, NY. Established in 2017, the Farm focuses on the global administration of copyrights. We develop meaningful relationships with performance and mechanical societies, seek out synchronization opportunities, and administer production music.

    In a fast-paced world of constant technological change, the Farm is a throwback to the older days of music publishers. Here, our people answer phones, develop meaningful relationships with writers and have a deep knowledge of the music in our catalog. The Farm is familiar with the international landscape, popular music in different countries, the major players in the industry, and the sync and advertising landscape.

    We’re open to songwriters of all shapes and sizes, all styles and time periods. Our catalog is diverse, spanning multiple genres across several decades, and dating back to the 1950s. The catalog’s earliest compositions were recorded by iconic easy listening artists, such as Perry Como and the Ray Charles Singers. Newer additions include more contemporary styles such as hip hop, EDM, and indie rock.

    Trivia

    The Farm is located in the neighborhood which was originally part of the Noah Tompkins Farm in the Village of Mamaroneck, NY. Noah Tompkins was a member of one of the earliest families to settle in Westchester County, NY, and his farm dates back to 1644. Some folks in our neighborhood believe that our office is located in the original Noah Tompkins farm house.
