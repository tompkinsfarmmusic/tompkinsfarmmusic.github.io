ABOUT US

Tompkins Farm Music is a boutique New York-based music publisher with a global mindset. We focus on worldwide administration, licensing and placements of music in film, television and advertising. Our catalog is diverse and includes exceptional songwriters from the 1940s to the present who have contributed to the Great American Songbook and popular culture.
