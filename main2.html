welcome to the farm!

songwriter-first approach

We focus on the best moves for writers to grow, and a personalized approach yields much better results. Writers get individual attention from the owner of the company, who knows the songs inside and out; we understand everyone has their own unique path to take.

unmatched communication

Most publishers have cast aside real interaction in favor of automation, resulting in administrators not even knowing the songs in their catalog. Better decisions come from understanding the music and writers. At Tompkins Farm, songwriters always have direct contact with the owner of the company. Transparency and trust are keys to success!

effortless registration

Registering your works with collection agencies is convoluted and time-consuming, and you should be able to use that time on what really matters: your art. Let us handle it for you.

maximize your revenue

Digital service providers are the name of the game in the 2020's, but too often songwriters only see a fraction of the revenue they could be collecting. We can obtain these lost funds for you, increasing your revenue for every stream!

reach film music supervisors

The importance of making your songs easy to find for supervisors cannot be overstated. We have close relationships with award-winning music supervisors and coordinators, and exclusive access to state-of-the-art song databases!

about us

what is tompkins farm music?

Tompkins Farm Music is a boutique music publisher located in Westchester County, NY. Established in 2017, the Farm focuses on the global administration of copyrights. We develop meaningful relationships with performance and mechanical societies, seek out synchronization opportunities, and administer production music.

In a fast-paced world of constant technological change, the Farm is a throwback to the older days of music publishers. Here, our people answer phones, develop meaningful relationships with writers and have a deep knowledge of the music in our catalog. The Farm is familiar with the international landscape, popular music in different countries, the major players in the industry, and the sync and advertising landscape.

trivia

The Farm is located in the neighborhood which was originally part of the Noah Tompkins Farm in the Village of Mamaroneck, NY. Noah Tompkins was a member of one of the earliest families to settle in Westchester County, NY, and his farm dates back to 1644. Some folks in our neighborhood believe that our office is located in the original Noah Tompkins farm house.
